About:

Close Podcast is a midnight conversation between two close friends Nishal (Right Side) & Dilli Hang (Left Side) discussing various topics like Spirituality, Failures/Success, and Trekking from life experiences.

Disclaimer: We are not experts and are not intentionally criticizing any particular individual. It is an unfiltered normal causal chat and talk from our up-to-date knowledge, experiences, and thought processes. We share our experiences, thoughts, OPINIONS, and knowledge. Amid our discussions, we often find ourselves caught in the overwhelmed moment, sharing real and unfiltered thoughts.

One of the reasons for this podcast is for a memory snapshot of our evolving ideas, experiences, and shared moments as well as a mark of our growth and learning.
